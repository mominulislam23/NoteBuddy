beowulf allegory, heroic poem

Allegory: Allegory is a form of extended metaphor in which objects and persons within a narrative are equated with meanings that lie outside the narrative. It is a type of writing that has hidden meanings, usually political or moral ones. According to J.D. Cuddon the term allegory is “any writing in verse or prose that has a double meaning”.
